# PRIVACY POLICY — PartyFix

**Last updated: May 12, 2026**

---

## 1. Who we are

PartyFix is an Android application developed and maintained by an individual developer. For questions, you can contact us at partyfix@proton.me.

---

## 2. What data we collect

PartyFix is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personal data on external servers. All data used by the app is stored exclusively on your own device. This includes:

- **Location coordinates:** When you manually save a location (e.g. your tent or car), we store the latitude and longitude on your device.
- **Photos:** Photos you take within the app of your saved spots are stored in the app's secure local storage.
- **Preferences:** Your favourite events, planning, theme colour, and app settings.
- **Cached event data:** Event information is temporarily stored locally so the app remains usable offline.

We have no access to this data, do not sell it, and do not use any tracking or advertising networks.

---

## 3. Use of Firebase

PartyFix uses Firebase by Google LLC to send push notifications. All optional data collection is disabled. No personal data is collected or received by us. More information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 4. Internet connection

The app connects to the following sources to load content:

- **DJGuide:** To load the latest festival data on a daily basis.
- **Ticketmaster API:** For up-to-date event information. Event data is temporarily cached on your device.
- **Tidal:** Only when you actively use the button to look up an artist.
- **Spotify:** Only when you actively use the button to look up an artist.

---

## 5. Affiliate links

Some ticket links in PartyFix are affiliate links. If you purchase a ticket through such a link, we may receive a small commission. This does not result in any additional cost to you.

---

## 6. Permissions

To enable the app's features, we request the following permissions:

- **Location (Precise Location):** Required to determine your current position relative to your saved spots (Finder), calculate distances, and display the compass arrow.
- **Camera:** Used when you want to add a photo to a saved location in the Finder.
- **Internet access:** To load the festival feed and images.
- **Notifications:** To send you reminders for acts in your personal planning.

---

## 7. Data shared by the user

You can choose to share a saved location (including coordinates and photo) with third parties via the 'Share' button (e.g. via WhatsApp). PartyFix never shares data automatically; this only happens at your own initiative.

---

## 8. Children

PartyFix is intended for users aged 18 and older.

---

## 9. Data deletion

Users have full control over their data in PartyFix. Since all data is stored exclusively on your device, you can delete your data in the following ways:

- **Individual data:** In the 'Finder' screen, you can manually delete each saved location and its associated photo by tapping the trash icon.
- **All data:** You can delete all data stored by the app (settings, favourites, locations, photos, and cached event data) at once by uninstalling the app from your device, or by clearing the app's 'Storage/Cache' via Android settings.

We do not store any of your data on our servers, so there is simply nothing to delete on our end.

---
